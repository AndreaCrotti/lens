at :: Int -> SimpleIndexedLens Int (IntMap v) (Maybe v)Source

This Lens can be used to read, write or delete the value associated with a key in an IntMap. 

>>> fromList [(1,"hello")] ^.at 1
Just "hello"

>>> at 1 .~ Just "hello" $ IntMap.empty
fromList [(1,"hello")]
